Frequently asked questions

We have given answers to the most popular questions below

01

Is my contact data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to ensure that your personal and professional details are secure. You have full control over what information is visible on your digital business card and who can access it.
02

Can I export my connections to Salesforce or Hubspot?

Absolutely. Eventzone supports direct integrations with major CRMs including Salesforce, Hubspot, and Pipedrive. You can also export your connections as a standard CSV file at any time.
03

Do people need the app to scan my card?

No! Anyone with a standard smartphone camera can scan your Eventzone QR code. It will open a responsive web profile where they can save your contact details directly to their phone's address book.
04

Can I create multiple profiles for different businesses?

Yes, with our Pro plan you can create and manage multiple profiles from a single account. Easily switch between your main business, side hustle, or freelancer profile with just a tap.
05

Is there a physical card option?

Absolutely. While our app provides a complete digital experience, you can also order a custom-designed NFC-enabled metal or plastic card that links directly to your Eventzone profile.
