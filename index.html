Frequently asked questions

We have given answers to the most popular questions below

01

Is my contact data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to ensure that your personal and professional details are secure. You have full control over what information is visible on your digital business card and who can access it.
02

Do people need the app to scan my card?

No! Anyone with a standard smartphone camera can scan your Eventzone QR code. It will open a responsive web profile where they can save your contact details directly to their phone's address book.
03

Can I customize the design of my digital business card?

Yes, Eventzone allows you to fully personalize your card. You can choose custom colors, upload your profile picture, and add your social media links to match your personal brand perfectly.
04

How do I share my Eventzone profile with someone?

Sharing is effortless. You can let them scan your unique QR code directly from the app, or send your personalized profile link via text, email, or any social media platform.
05

Is there a limit to how many links I can add to my profile?

No limits! You can add as many contact methods, websites, portfolios, and social media profiles as you need to showcase your complete professional identity.
